    Format:

    1) Each student has 10 minutes to do the Oral Test individually. Please pick up a time slot for the Final Oral Exam. This sign-up form is available on Canvas.

    2) The Final Oral Exam has two parts:

    Part I. Text Reading (50%)

    You will be asked to read aloud one text from the following choices: L8 A Letter, L9 - Dialogue 1, L9 - Dialogue 2. Please prepare and be familiar with all of them.

     

    Part II. Q & A with your instructor (50%) 

    After you finish reading  the  text, you  will  be  asked  a  few  questions based on the content that you have learned in CHIN 0100. The topics are related to campus life and your own situation. Below is the Question Bank.
